Twitter Sentiment Analysis for Ukraine-Russia Conflict

Analyzing the Sentiment Distribution of Tweets about the War in Ukraine - An in depth Analysis

On the 24th of February, 2022, Russia invaded Ukraine. Inspite of a lot of speculations about the possible invasion, it has been surprising to see a full scale invasion in the 21st century. This analysis reads thousands of tweets by people all over the world and analyzes their sentiments related to the war.
